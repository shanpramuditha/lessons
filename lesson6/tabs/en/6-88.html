Growth is influenced by


  • Biological determinants including sex

  • Intrauterine environment

  • Birth order

  • Birth weight

  • Parental size and genetic constitution

  • Environment factors, including climate, season and socio- economic levels (includes presents or absents of infective, parasitic and psychological illness),and above all by the level of nutrition.
