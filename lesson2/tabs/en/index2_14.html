Guidelines for a healthy and balanced lifestyle

  • Eat a variety of food daily which are clean and safe.Eat a combination of different food from different food groups which provide the right quantity of macro and micronutrients

  • Choose a diet with plenty of vegetables, fruits and root crops. Eat fruit and vegetables of a variety of colours to get a range of different nutrients.Eat food containing fat and sugar sparingly

  • Consume milk, dairy products and other calcium rich food such as small fish and dark green leafy vegetables daily

  • Choose a diet low in fat and cholesterol. Choose white meat (e.g. chicken, fish), which is generally lower in fat, rather than red meat

  • Drink plenty of water (children 1-1.5L per day and adults 1.5-2L per day)

  • try to incorporate green leafy vegetables in your daily diet

  • reduce the consumption of fast food and junk food and eat home-prepared food as much as possible

  •   be active indulge in sports, play, walking for at least 30-45 minutes daily

  •   maintain the right weight for your height

  • Don’t skip your meals (especially breakfast). Have small meals more frequently
