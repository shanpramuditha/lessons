• Summary

සාරාංශය

  •   It is important to consume a balance diet containing all required nutrients in adequate amounts. Too little or excess consumption of some nutrients over time may cause dietary deficiency or toxicity and eventually lead to contracting a diseases.

  •   Protein Energy Malnutrition (PEM), Iron Deficiency Anaemia (IDA) and Iodine Deficiency Disorders (IDD) are the most common under-nutrition problems affecting the community. Young children are the most vulnerable group to these diseases.

  •   Excess or imbalanced consumption of some nutrients would lead to another set of diseases. Diabetes, hypertension, heart diseases are some cancers belong to this category along with nutrition problems like overweight and obesity.

  •   Adherence to healthy dietary guidelines and lifestyle practices would reduce the occurrence of nutrition related diseases in the community.
