Reasons for PEM

1. Insufficient nutrition in the diet is caused by:

  • Inadequate energy
  • Low quantity and quality of protein
  • Inadequate vitamin and minerals

2. Frequent infections

Infection and malnutrition are interrelated and one condition aggravates the other.

The link between infection and malnutrition is a vicious cycle

but may be broken with proper nutrition and treatment.

Under-nutrition is a major public health issue in Sri Lanka as shown by National Nutrition Survey data from 1993-2006
