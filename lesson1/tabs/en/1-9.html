Junk food contains more energy in relation to other nutrients. They often contain high amounts of fat or carbohydrates. Since they do not provide important nutrients in adequate quantities, they are considered unhealthy. Hence frequent consumption should be avoided.

Energy yielding nutrients


The average energy content of the three macro nutrients:

  • Carbohydrate- - 4 kcal/g

  • Protein -4 kcal/g

  • Fat -9 kcal/g

Where should energy come from?


The energy in uor daily diet is provided by the three macronutrients: fats, carbohydrates and proteins.
