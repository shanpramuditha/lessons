Summary


  • Food provides nutrients and energy required for growth, physical activity and long term health.

  • Food can be categorized according to their function and their food group.

  • Having a variety of food in your diet is essential.

  • The food guide pyramid approach can be used to have a healthy and a balances diet. We must be conscious of the quantity of different food groups we eat. (E.g. portion sizes)

  • We must have a balanced diet to ensure that we obtain the right amount of energy and nutrients at the right time.
