The different food groups


For convenience of study, the food we eat is categorized into several groups. Since each food group contains similar nutrients, a general statement can be made regarding the nutrients provided by them.

Cereals


  • Mainly provide energy, protein, B-vitamins, fiber (if the whole grain is consumed) and minerals like iron & calcium E.g. Rice. Wheat, maize, kurakkan, sorghum, barley

Pulses and legumes


  • Rich in proteins ( alternative protein source for vegetarians)

  • Rich in B-vitamins, minerals and fiber

  • Rich in antioxidants and have less fat.

    E.g. Beans, lentils, green gram, chickpea, black gram, soya

Fish, meat and their products

  •       A good source of protein and provides energy
  •       Provides vitamins and minerals (haeme iron, zinc)
  •       Fish with omega-3 fatty acids are heart friendly
  • Red meat (beef, lamb, mutton) are high in protein  
  • White meat (chicken, pork)

Eggs

  • A good quality source of protein and also a source of fat, vitamins and minerals.
  • An egg York contains about 200mg cholesterol.
