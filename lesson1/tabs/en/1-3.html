What are nutrients?


Nutrients are the constituents in food that facilitate essential body functions (energy giving, body building and regulatory). The body requires nutrients supplied in adequate amounts for healthy functioning.

What is wellness?


Wellness is enhanced health maintained by a good diet, exercise and a healthy lifestyle combined with a feeling of vitality. Wellness is a state of equilibrium on the three dimensions of balance, understanding and pleasure.

Wellness is the base on which well-being is built. Well- being is practicing a lifestyle that helps us safeguard and even improve our health. It is often illustrated by the balanced combination of 5 important aspects of daily life: Social, Emotional, Physical, Spiritual and intellectual well-being.
