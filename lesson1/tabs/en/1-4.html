Different food categories have different benefits


Food can be broadly categorized into three groups depending on the most important functions they are involved with.

Energy- rich food


Food containing mainly carbohydrates and/or fat provides the energy needed to grow, to maintain body warmth and for physical activities.



Examples for carbohydrate-rich food include cereals such as rice, wheat, maize, kurakkan, sorghum; starchy root crop such as manioc, potato, sweet potato and other yams; jackfruit; sugar, jiggery, treacle etc.


Examples for fat-rich food include butter, margarine, avocado, coconut oil and other vegetable oils such as corn oil, soya bean oil, sunflower oil etc.
