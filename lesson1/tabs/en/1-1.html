FOOD AND NUTRIENTS

OBJECTIVE


To increase knowledge on the fundamentals of nutrition

LEARNING OUTCOMES 


At the end of the module students will be able to

  • Describe the importance of food
  • Identify the major nutrients found in different types of food
  • Apply the food pyramid approach in selecting types and proportion of food consumption
  • Prepare a balanced diet from a given set of food categoties

OVERVIEW

We eat food for many reasons but mainly to survive and satisfy our hunger and thirst. Food provides energy and nutrients that are required for the body’s growth, performance and maintenance. Inappropriate food choices, at different stages in our life may cause inadequacies, imbalance or an excess of nutrients which may have an adverse effect on our bodies. Food is a combination of nutrients. The proportion of nutrients present in food will vary widely. Food containing too much of one nutrient (e.g. fat) or too little of another (e.g. Protein) are bad for our health. Thus we should learn more about food, its nutrient content and their functions in order to make the correct food choices (a balanced diet) to enjoy a healthy and active life.
