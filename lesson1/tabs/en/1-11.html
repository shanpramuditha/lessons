The food guide pyramid


In order to grow, be healthy and active it is important that we make correct food choices to get the right amount of energy and nutrients.

The quantitative guide about eating different types of food is given in the food guide pyramid. By incorporating the principles of balance, variety and moderation, we can still eat our favourite food by following the food guide pyramid.
