Spices as natural remedies for some common diseases


Spices are wonderful ingredients which make food delicious and increase appetite. In addition, according to recent scientific findings, spices have shown extraordinary healing power and disease preventive potential.

Thus you should think twice before you throw away curry leaves (“karapincha”) from your plate. Sri Lanka producers some of the world’s finest spices such as cinnamon, ginger, cloves etc.

Cinnamon


  •       Cinnamon has an outstanding ability to tackle diabetes by boosting insulin function. Much of its health benefits are retained primarily in its water soluble form and not in the oil.

  •       In addition, cinnamon is a good antioxidant, antibacterial, anti-inflammatory agent. It is known to help reduce blood pressure, cholesterol and even body fat.
