To prevent food poisoning, you must consider the following steps when preparing food:


  • Carefully wash your hands and clean dishes and utensils

  • DO NOT use outdated (expired) food, packaged food with a broken seal, or cans that are bulging or have a dent

  • DO NOT use food that has an unusual odor or an off taste

  • DO NOT eat unknown mushrooms

  • When travelling, you may encounter places where contamination of food is more likely, so try to eat hot, freshly cooked food, and drink clean, safe water (preferably boiled water)
