Garlic


  •       Garlic’s outstanding health benefits are due to its high content of Sulphur – bearing compound such as allicin.

  •       The most important beneficial properties of garlic are its antibiotic, anticancerous, blood thinning, antiviral and antifungal propeties that enable it to fight against high blood pressure, high cholesterol, arthritis, diabetes, influenza, leprosy, tuberculosis or even Aids.

  •       It is believed that, in order to enjoy the full benefits of garlic, it should be eaten raw. E.g. Add a few cloves of garlic to coconut sambol.

Ginger


      Ginger contains antiviral, antitoxic, and antifungal properties and is used for the prevention/ treatment of the common cold. It is proven (in multiple studies) to successfully treat nausea, particularly in the form of seasickness. E.g. Adding ginger into “lunukanda” when treating diarrhea.
