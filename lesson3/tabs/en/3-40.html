• නොකළ යුතු දේ

You must NEVER:


  • Touch, serve or eat food without washing your hands
  • Forget to wash your hands after using the toilet
  • Spit in public places
  • Blow and breathe over food which are intended to be served to others
  • Lick fingers or bite nails
  • Taste food by dipping your fingers into a dish
  • Touch food which is on display in your school canteen
  • Serve or offer any food to anyone who is unfit or suspicious to you
  • Leave food, garbage bins or drinking water uncovered
  • Lick your finger tips to make it easier to separate flimsy materials, e.g. turning pages in your book
