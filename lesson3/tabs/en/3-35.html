Food contamination and hygiene


  • Food contamination is a major factor affecting food safety. Contamination refer to any substance in food that makes it harmful.

  • Food hygiene refer to all conditions and measures necessary to guarantee the safety and suitability of food at all stages and ensures that it is safe for human consumption. Unhygienic food not only threatens your health, but may even cause death.

  • Food contamination occurs in numerous ways through files, cockroaches, rats, birds, improperly cleaned utensils etc. Unwashed/ dirty hands can carry millions of bacteria and other contaminants.

  • Carelessness and negligence promote food contamination which is easily preventable. It is a good habit and practice to wash hands before preparation and eating of food.
