Protect food preparation areas from pests

Pests are rats, birds, cockroaches, files and other insects. Your pet animals (dog, cats, birds, etc.) may also hots some pests such as ticks and fleas etc. pests/ pets may carry microorganisms on their feet, hair, fur and feathers.

To avoid contamination you should:

  • Keep food covered or in closed containers
  • Keep garbage bins covered and remove the garbage regularly
  • Keep food preparation areas in good condition
  • Use bait to get rid of pests
  • Keep domestic animals away from food preparation areas

Unhygienic habits and poor practices

Unhygienic habits and practices that you may be used to may cause severe health problems. For instance, as a habit you may wash your hands before eating but you may not always wash your hands before serving food to others. Remember, such habits badly affect the health of others as well.
