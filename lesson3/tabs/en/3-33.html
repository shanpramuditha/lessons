FOOD SAFETY AND HYGIENE FOOD SAFETY AND HYGIENE

OBJECTIVE


To increase awareness of the fundamentals of food and hygiene.

LEARNING OUTCOMES


At the end of the module students will be able to:

  •       Identify bad habits and poor practices associated with food
  •       Explain the importance of Personal hygiene in maintaining food safety
  •       Apply good healthcare practices at school and home
  •       List common, potentially hazardous, natural and induced toxins present in food
  • අසාත්මිකකාරක ගොනු කිරීම හා අසාත්මික තත්ත්ව වළකා ගැනීමට ගතයුතු ක්‍රියාමාර්ග යෝජනා කිරීම.
  •       List food allergens and suggest remedial measures for allergic problems

OVERVIEW


We have discussed the importance of a balanced diet in our day-to-day life in the previous two modules. It is equally important to ensure that we understand and practice the fundamentals of personal hygiene and food safety. Children like you are always active and curious to explore the world by touching and inspecting everything around you. However, less attention is paid to hygiene and health care unless persistently learn the fundamentals of personal hygiene and food safety. The focus of this module is to make you vigilant about food safety and persuade you to follow correct hygienic practices when dealing with food.
