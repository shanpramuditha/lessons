Summary


  • Food contamination is a major factor affecting food safety. Contamination by microorganism are the most frequent and most dangerous.
  • Food hygiene refer to all conditions and measures necessary to ensure the safety and suitability of food until it is consumed.
  • Food safety means the protection of health and well-being of people by safeguarding food from anything that could cause health hazard.
  • There are three major factors which make food unsafe for human consumption – they can be categorized as biological factors, chemical factors and physical factors.
  • It is a good habit and practice to wash hands before food preparation, eating and serving food
  • It is better to be aware of potentially hazardous natural toxins and toxins induced due to poor cooking practices.
  • Cooking oil should not be reused for frying food since this could generate some harmful chemicals in the oil.
  • Food poisoning incidents are mainly caused by various types of bacteria which are not seen by the naked eye.
  • For drinking purposes and cleaning of food and hands, always use clean water.
  • Food allergy can be defined as an adverse immune-mediate reaction to food.
  • Spices possess extraordinary healing power and disease preventive potential.
