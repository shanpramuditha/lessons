To wash your hands properly you must:


  • As a habit, you should cut and keep your nails as short as possible. Use a nail brush to clean between your finger nails.
  • Always use soap and clean water
  • Wet your hands properly before applying soap
  • Rub your hands together for at least 20 seconds with soap
  • Apply soap not only around the hands but between the fingers and fingertips
  • Use a brush to clean your nails
  • Clean the back of your hands and wrists
  • Wash your hands thoroughly with running water to completely remove any greasy feeling
  • Dry hand thoroughly with a clean dry towel. Don’t use dirty napkins / cloth or your clothes
  • Do not pierce your hands with your hands.
