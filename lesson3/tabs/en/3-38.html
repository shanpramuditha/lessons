Good hygiene ensures food safety


මුළු තැන්ගෙයි භාජන, උපකරණ ආදිය පවිත කිරිමේදී පහත කරුණු පිළිබඳ ඔබ සැලකිලිමත් විය යුතුය.

During food preparations

  • Thoroughly clean the cutting boards and utensils with soap or disinfectant after they have been contact with raw meat/ fish or seafood
  • You should pay more attention to the used dishes with raw food (Eg: green vegetables, meat and eggs).

    • Pay special attention to eating, drinking and cooking utensils that touch raw food or the mouth

After meals

  • Scrape excess food in to a garbage bin
  • Wash in water (ideally hot water) with soap or detergent, using a clean cloth or brush to remove left over food and oil
  • Rinse in clean water
  • Sanitize (destruction of disease causing microorganisms) utensils with boiling water or with sanitizing solution. Sanitize feeding aids like milk feeding bottles and teats that are vulnerable to contamination with boiling water or a suitable sanitizing solution. (e.g. chemicals or hot water which has the capacity to destroy microorganisms)
  • Leave dishes and cooking utensils to air – dry or wipe them with a clean dry cloth
  • Do not forget to clean and dry cloths, sponges, brushes etc.; which have been used for cleaning purposes
