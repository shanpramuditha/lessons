Poor food preparation practices

  • Washing vegetables after cutting will reduce a significant amount of vitamin C. water soluble vitamins are highly vulnerable to loss during washing or draining of water. Thoroughly clean the exterior of such products to retain water soluble nutrients.

  • පහසුවෙන් නරක් විමට තුඩු දෙන ආහාර (උදා :- මාළු ,මස් )අයිස් දියකර හැරිමෙන් අධි ශිත කිරිමත් නැවත නැවත නොකළ යුතුය. මෙය සෞඛළුට අහිතකර තත්ත්ව ගෙන දිය හැකිය.

Why?

Freezing does not completely kill microorganism. When you leave frozen food to melt its ice completely (thawing), microorganisms remaining in it can grow faster and multiply due to increasing temperature.If you freeze food for the second time, there will be more microorganisms in the food. The microbial load will increase every time you thaw a frozen product, increasing the risk of spoilage and food poisoning.

During freezing tiny ice crystals are formed inside the muscle tissue, which pierces it. While fish or meat is frozen, liquids won’t ooze out. However during the thawing process, water soluble nutrients leach out (drip loss). Drip loss not only leads to a nutrition loss, but may provide nutrients for growing microbes.
